About CBS 2026

Where Biological Science Meets Innovation

The Conference on Biological Sciences (CBS 2026) is a premier international forum hosted by the University of Buea, bringing together leading scientists, researchers, and policymakers to share cutting-edge discoveries across the life sciences.

CBS 2026 covers the full spectrum of biological inquiry — from molecular biology and genetics to ecology, conservation, and biotechnology. The programme features keynote addresses, parallel thematic sessions, poster exhibitions, workshops, and rich networking opportunities.
