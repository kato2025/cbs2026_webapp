About the Conference

Discover the vision, mission, and people behind the Conference on Biological Sciences 2026 at the University of Buea, Cameroon.

Our Mission

Where Biological Science Meets Innovation

The Conference on Biological Sciences (CBS 2026) is a premier international scientific forum hosted by the University of Buea. It brings together leading scientists, researchers, graduate students, and policymakers to share cutting-edge discoveries and foster collaborative breakthroughs across the life sciences.

From molecular biology and genetics to ecology, conservation, and biotechnology — CBS 2026 covers the full spectrum of biological inquiry. The conference features keynote addresses by internationally distinguished scholars, parallel thematic sessions, poster exhibitions, workshops, and networking opportunities.

CBS 2026 places a special emphasis on African biodiversity, tropical biology, and the unique biological and health challenges facing the African continent — while maintaining global scientific standards and fostering international collaboration.

Our Pillars

Conference Values

Research Excellence

We uphold the highest standards of scientific rigour, welcoming original, peer-reviewed research that advances human knowledge.

Collaboration

We believe in the transformative power of cross-border, interdisciplinary partnerships that multiply the impact of individual discoveries.

African Biodiversity

Central Africa hosts some of the world's richest ecosystems. CBS 2026 spotlights research on and by African scientists.

Capacity Building

Empowering emerging researchers and students is central to our mission, through mentorship, awards, and dedicated sessions.

Global Inclusion

Hybrid attendance ensures researchers from every corner of the world can participate, regardless of resources or location.

Open Science

We promote open-access publication, data sharing, and transparency as cornerstones of responsible science.
