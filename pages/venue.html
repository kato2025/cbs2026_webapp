Conference Venue

Venue & Travel

CBS 2026 is hosted at the University of Buea, Cameroon — nestled at the foot of the iconic Mount Cameroon.

Open in Google Maps
Location Details

University of Buea

Main Venue

University of Buea Main Auditorium & Faculty of Science Conference Halls, Molyko, Buea, South West Region, Cameroon

Natural Setting

Located at the foot of Mount Cameroon (4,095m) — Central Africa's highest active volcano and a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve.

Nearest Airport

Douala International Airport (DLA) — approximately 70 km, 1.5–2 hours by road. Airport taxis and agency buses available.

Accommodation

A curated list of recommended hotels and guesthouses at various price points will be sent to all registered participants.

Facilities

High-speed internet, AV presentation systems, hybrid conferencing infrastructure, on-site catering, and parking.

Visa & Entry

Participants requiring invitation letters for visa applications should contact kato.namuene@ubuea.cm at least 6 weeks before the conference.
