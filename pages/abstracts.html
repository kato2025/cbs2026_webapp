Call for Contributions

Abstract Submission

Submit your original research for consideration as an oral or poster presentation. Accepted abstracts will be published in the conference proceedings.

How to Submit

Submission Guide

01

Prepare Your Abstract

Structured format: Background, Methods, Results, Conclusion. Max 300 words, written in English.

02

Complete the Form

Fill in all author details, select your thematic track, and paste your abstract text.

03

Receive Reference ID

You will receive an abstract reference ID and acknowledgement by email on submission.

04

Await Decision

The scientific committee will notify you of its decision by 31 October 2026.

Submission Rules

  • ✓Max 300 words, English only
  • ✓Structured: Background · Methods · Results · Conclusion
  • ✓Include 3–5 keywords (semicolon-separated)
  • ✓No tables, figures, or references in abstract text
  • ✓All authors listed with affiliations
  • ✓Presenting author must register for the conference
  • ✓Max 2 oral submissions per presenting author
