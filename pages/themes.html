Thematic Tracks

CBS 2026 covers 12 broad disciplines in the biological sciences. Submit your abstract to the track that best fits your research.

Scientific Programme

All Thematic Areas

Explore the full breadth of biological disciplines covered at CBS 2026. Authors may submit to the track that best fits their work.

Track 01

Molecular Biology & Genetics

Gene expression, genomics, proteomics, CRISPR, epigenetics, and molecular mechanisms of disease.

Track 02

Ecology & Environmental Biology

Ecosystem dynamics, biodiversity conservation, climate change impacts, and environmental sustainability.

Track 03

Microbiology & Infectious Disease

Pathogen biology, antimicrobial resistance, tropical diseases, virology, and host-pathogen interactions.

Track 04

Biochemistry & Cell Biology

Metabolic pathways, cell signalling, enzyme kinetics, structural biology, and cellular mechanisms.

Track 05

Plant Biology & Botany

Plant physiology, ethnobotany, phytochemistry, agrobiology, and plant-microbe interactions.

Track 06

Zoology & Animal Sciences

Vertebrate and invertebrate biology, comparative physiology, animal behaviour, and wildlife science.

Track 07

Neuroscience & Physiology

Neurobiology, sensory systems, hormonal regulation, and integrative physiology.

Track 08

Biotechnology & Bioinformatics

Computational biology, bioengineering, systems biology, and translational research applications.

Track 09

Aquatic & Marine Biology

Freshwater ecology, marine biodiversity, fisheries biology, and aquatic toxicology.

Track 10

Public Health & One Health

Zoonoses, epidemiology, vector biology, and the intersection of human, animal, and environmental health.

Track 11

Structural & Evolutionary Biology

Phylogenetics, speciation, palaeobiology, developmental biology, and evolutionary theory.

Track 12

Food Biology & Nutrition Science

Functional foods, food safety, nutritional biochemistry, gut microbiome, and food security research.
